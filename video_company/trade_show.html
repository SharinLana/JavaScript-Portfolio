What is a Trade Show Video?

This is a fully edited video about your products or services you presented at a trade show. The fastest way to demonstrate the effectiveness and quality of your goods and consumer demand for it, as well as remind of yourself to regular customers through social media.

How does it work?

Our film crew will arrive at your location to shoot your exhibition space focusing on the most important products from your collection. We will also film the atmosphere of the show.

The result.

You will get an edited Full HD video about your participation in the show within 2 weeks after video recording. The video file will be emailed to you via a download link. You will receive all rights to the video and will be able to dispose of it at your discretion after making full payment. For more information, please contact us by email: showmeshow@yahoo.com
