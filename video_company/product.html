What is a Product Video?

This type of video can help you sell your goods on an online store by demonstrating features of the products that cannot be conveyed through photography. With this video, your online shoppers can get an idea of the size of the product they are interested in, its consistency, texture, flexibility, and more.

How does it work?

Filming and editing takes place in our studio in Henderson, Nevada, USA. You can send us your products by mail, and we will make a short video about each item within 2 - 3 weeks after receiving the goods.

The result.

Depending on your needs and budget, you can get one or several short product videos of varying degrees of technical complexity. For more information, please contact our executive producer Lana by email: showmeshow@yahoo.com
