What is a Commercial Video?

Commercial video is a type of advertising, the purpose of which is to form or improve the image of a company, to sell a service or product.

How does it work?

You send us your ideas for commercial videos as storyboards. We review them and make a rough estimate. After discussing all the details of the process and adjusting the scripts, we hire actors / models, support staff, rent premises for video filming and proceed to what we love most in our life - video filming and editing.

The result.

Within the agreed period (usually a month after the end of filming), you receive the first versions of commercial videos of Full HD or 4K quality. We also provide one round of edits for free. You will get all rights to use the video as you see fit after making full payment for the project. For more information, please contact us by email: showmeshow@yahoo.com
