Change Focus Reminder

The purpose of this app is to remind you to look away from your computer screen from time to time to keep your eyesight healthy.

Ophthalmologists recommend changing focus from close to far at least once every 20 minutes. But you can set your own frequency of reminders.

Just enter the time interval at which you would like to be reminded to change focus. And the application will remind you of this during the entire time of your work at the computer.
